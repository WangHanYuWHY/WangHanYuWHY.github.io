Unmanned store

Walking into the unattended shop, the consumer directly takes the merchandise on the shelf, and after walking out of the store, the wallet in the phone shows the total price and automatically settles the bill.This is the world's hottest unmanned economic issue and drives the revolution in the retail industry – the unmanned store.

Pilotless automobile

Unmanned driving is actually a human-like driving, that is, the computer mimics the driving behavior of the human driver. The goal is to make the computer a fast-moving, full-spirited, experienced, never-tiring virtual driver, which ultimately makes humans from low-level, cumbersome and lasting.Liberated from the driving activities.

Intelligent health management

Collection, transmission and utilization of intelligent devices are wearable devices.
